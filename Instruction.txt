2) Quality
3) consumer



what data input is done at start
1) Qty
2) itemnumber
3) item type

5) Description


what are the types in management of material in the system
1) raw
2) finished goods
   1) External direct goods
   2) Internal after completion of production



Current Process

Placing Stocks

case1:

raw material

1) when the delivery is received it has delivery note, the logistics enters the data in erp and book the stock
2) create the label sticker for the box, and add the goods to that box
3) place the box in the shelf


case2:

finished goods

External direct goods

-Receive delivery and delivery note, logistics enters the data to erp and book the stock.
-create the label sticker for the box, and add the goods to that box
-Quality check Department check that
    - if good ,Yes, then later the quality department will transfer to the location and put into shelf
    - if bad, No, then go to the separate section of the warehouse and placed in spare shelf and declared block


2.  Internal after completion of production

After production the parts goes to separate section of the plant, waits for the logistics to approach and then as there will be already the number on the production order to final location they have to place over there after the check.


Consumption of Stock

-There is only two logic
    - either consumed
    - stock place to stock shift
